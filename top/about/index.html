Damien (@damienomurchu) is a software engineer at Red Hat with a passion for developing applications.

He is also an active and enthusiastic participant in outreach and educational programmes as a mentor and a teacher.

When he is not working he enjoys watching movies, low-tech fitness traning, travelling, writing and learning languages.
